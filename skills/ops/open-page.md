---
title: Open Page
summary: Open a specific page on our website and show a short summary
tags: tool, navigation
prerequisites: []
---

Use this tool when the user wants more detailed information.

Example:
User: "Tell me more about living in Haiphong"
Bot: "Would you like me to open the full guide about living in Haiphong?"
User: "Yes"
Bot: Opens /guides/living-in-haiphong and shows a short summary in chat.

Do not open pages without user confirmation.
